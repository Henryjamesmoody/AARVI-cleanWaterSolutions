About AARVI Clean Water Solutions

Founded in 2024 as a proprietorship, AARVI CLEAN WATER SOLUTION is owned by Mr. Suraj Namdev Gadsing and managed by Mr. Ankit Sanjay More. We specialize in providing end‑to‑end fabrication services for Water Treatment Plants (WTP), Sewage Treatment Plants (STP), and civil construction projects. Our dedicated team fabricates Mild Steel (MS) and Stainless Steel (SS) products tailored to customer needs.

Mission-To deliver sustainable and cost‑effective water solution and fabrication services through engineering excellence, safety, and unwavering commitment to quality. Vision- To be India’s most trusted partner for fabricated water‑treatment infrastructure, leading the industry in innovation, reliability, and customer satisfaction.
